About The Festival

The festival is a two-day celebration which features keynotes, a Hackathon, panels, roundtables, workshops, and an NYU Startup Exhibition (feat. 60+ NYU Startups). All keynotes and panelists are NYU student, faculty, or alumni entrepreneurs.

This year's keynotes will be released soon!

Admission provides unlimited access to all talks, panels, and roundtables, refreshments on both days, breakfast + lunch on Saturday. 1000+ Attendees
