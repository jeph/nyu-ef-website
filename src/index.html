About The Festival

The NYU Entrepreneurs Festival is a weekend-long celebration of NYU’s thriving entrepreneurial community, offering inspiration and guidance to both budding and seasoned entrepreneurs.

Open to aspiring entrepreneurs, established business professionals, and anyone curious about entrepreneurship, The NYU Entrepreneurs Festival is the largest student-run event of its kind.
Through talks, workshops, & roundtables, over 1000 people gather to exchange ideas, lessons,
and inspiration.

Startups

The NYU Entrepreneurs Festival highlights the amazing startup community of both current and graduated NYU students. The alumni startup exhibition will be on Friday, while current students will display on Saturday. At the student exhibition, attendees will vote for their favorite startups to compete in The Pitch on Saturday night. The 3 startups with the most votes will pitch to a panel of venture capitalists for a chance to win $1,000 for their startup!

Idea Jam: The Future of Food

This three hour event, focused on innovation within the food industry, will enable attendees with various skills and interests to work in a team, simulating the ideation process of creating a startup as they brainstorm, refine, and pitch a concept. No technical experience is required. Please bring a laptop. Dinner will be served!

The Pitch

After our student startup exhibition on Saturday, attendees will have the opportunity to vote on their favorite student startup. The three teams with the most votes will pitch to our panel of venture capitalists for a chance to win $1000!
